ABOUT ME

I like to teach wires to talk. Hand me a sketchy idea, a fistful of sensors, and a deadline, and I’ll turn the whole mess into a circuit that chats with your phone before lunch. Eight years of poking at electrons have shown me that if you can’t explain a blinking LED to a curious teenager, you don’t understand it yet.

I roam from PCB traces to app screens—one minute routing copper so a microcontroller stays cool, the next crafting a clean mobile UI so a user can press a button and hear a relay click across the room.

My playground is the Internet of Things, where tiny gadgets gossip over Wi-Fi. I build these ecosystems to be secure, power-savvy, and courteous on the network.

When the soldering iron cools, I’m turning complex ideas into plain talk—tutorials, demos, and lively explanations that leave “complicated” at the door.
